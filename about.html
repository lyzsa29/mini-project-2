Historical Background

   Long before the coming of the Spaniards, the barrio of Capayang was quiet and isolated place. It was inhabited by few people. The beauty of the nature like green tall trees, green mountain and hills, river, and brooks with the clear and cold water which makes it a place grows abundantly everywhere. The barrio has also known name at that time the place got its name related by an old name who was the elderly inhabitants of the place.

   One time when our country was under the Spanish regimen, group of Spanish Soldiers made a visit to the place. As they were walking around, they passed by a tall papaya tree with green and yellow fruits. They saw all day picking papayas. The group approached a lady and ask what the name of this place is. The lady replied "Kapaya po" knowing that their asking for the name of the fruit. From that time on that place was called Capayang.

PANGLANTAW (VISION)

    "Isang Kumunidad na kumakatawan sa isang progresibong pag-unlad tungo sa magandang pagsusulong ng pagbabago sa larangan ng kalakalan, komersiyo at turismo na isinasaalang-alang ang kalikasan at sosyo-ekonomiko upang mabigyan ng magandang serbisyo at benepisyo ang mga mamamayan ng komunidad."

MISYON O TAHAS (MISSION)

   "Magtatag ng isang komunidad na may kahandaang tumulong at magbigay ng suporta sa mga mahahalaga at pangunahing pangangailanagng serbisyo ng mga mamamayan."
